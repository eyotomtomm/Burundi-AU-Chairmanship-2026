# Privacy Policy
## Burundi AU Chairmanship App

**Last Updated:** February 11, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the Burundi AU Chairmanship App ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app. This policy outlines our practices concerning the collection and use of your information.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Name:** Your full name as you provide it
- **Email Address:** Used for account identification and authentication
- **Password:** Stored securely using industry-standard encryption

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected

We may automatically collect:
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Usage Data:** Features you access, pages you view, time spent in the app
- **Log Data:** Error logs, crash reports for debugging purposes

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:
- Location data (unless you explicitly grant permission for embassy/event features)
- Contact list information
- Photos or media files (unless you choose to upload)
- Financial information (app is free, no payments)
- Browsing history from other apps or websites

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

### 3.1 Provide App Functionality
- Create and manage your account
- Authenticate your identity when you sign in
- Display personalized content and preferences
- Save your language and theme choices

### 3.2 Improve Our Services
- Analyze app usage to improve features
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features and functionality
- Optimize app performance

### 3.3 Communicate with You
- Send important updates about the app or services
- Respond to your support requests
- Notify you of changes to this Privacy Policy

### 3.4 Security and Fraud Prevention
- Detect and prevent unauthorized access
- Protect against security threats
- Maintain the integrity of our services

---

## 4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

### 4.1 Data Storage
- Your data is stored on secure servers
- We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission (SSL/TLS)
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Authentication uses JWT (JSON Web Tokens) for secure sessions

### 4.2 Data Security Measures
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls limiting who can view your data
- Secure coding practices to prevent vulnerabilities
- Regular backups to prevent data loss

### 4.3 Data Retention
- We retain your account data as long as your account is active
- Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days
- Backup data is purged according to our backup retention policy
- Log data is retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes

---

## 5. Sharing Your Information

### 5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### 5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

**With Your Consent:**
- When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information

**Service Providers:**
- Cloud hosting providers (for server infrastructure)
- Analytics services (anonymized usage statistics only)
- These providers are contractually bound to protect your data

**Legal Requirements:**
- To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to you)

### 5.3 Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services:
- **Embassy Locations:** Google Maps (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
- **External Links:** News articles, official government websites
- **Contact Features:** Email clients, phone dialers (controlled by your device)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access Your Data
You can:
- View your account information in the app's Profile section
- Request a copy of your data by contacting us

### 6.2 Update Your Data
You can:
- Edit your name and email in the Profile section
- Change your password at any time
- Update language and theme preferences

### 6.3 Delete Your Data
You have the right to:
- Delete your account at any time through the app (More > Delete Account)
- Request data deletion by contacting us directly
- Upon deletion, all your personal data is permanently removed

### 6.4 Withdraw Consent
You can:
- Opt out of optional data collection at any time
- Disable location services in your device settings
- Revoke permissions for camera, photos, etc.

### 6.5 Data Portability
You can request an export of your data in a machine-readable format.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

---

## 8. International Data Transfers

Our app is operated from Burundi. If you are accessing the app from outside Burundi, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Burundi or other countries where our service providers operate.

By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws.

---

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect:
- Changes in our practices
- New features or functionality
- Legal or regulatory requirements
- User feedback

When we make changes:
- We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top
- Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications
- Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## 10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 10.1 What We Use
- **Session Tokens:** To keep you logged in

### 10.2 What We Do NOT Use

## 11. Your Rights Under GDPR (If Applicable)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

- **Right to Access:** Request copies of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Request we limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Request data transfer to another service
- **Right to Object:** Object to our processing of your data
- **Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making:** We do not use automated decision-making

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

---

## 12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know:** What personal information we collect and how we use it
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** We do not sell personal information
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

---

## 13. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach that affects your personal information:
- We will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware
- Notification will include the nature of the breach and steps we're taking
- We will provide guidance on protective measures you can take
- We will cooperate with relevant authorities as required by law
