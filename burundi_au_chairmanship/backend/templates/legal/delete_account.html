Account Deletion Request

We respect your right to control your personal data. You can delete your B4africa account and all associated data at any time using the methods described below.

Method 1: Delete from the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly within the app:

  1. Open the B4africa app

    Launch the app and make sure you are logged in.

  2. Go to Profile

    Tap on the "More" tab, then tap your profile at the top.

  3. Scroll to "Delete Account"

    Scroll to the bottom of your profile page.

  4. Confirm deletion

    Tap "Delete Account" and confirm your choice. Your account will be scheduled for deletion.

Method 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

Send an email to support@burundi4africa.com with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your username (if known)

We will process your request within 48 hours.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

30-Day Grace Period: Your account is soft-deleted for 30 days. During this time, you can log back in to restore your account. After 30 days, all data is permanently and irreversibly deleted.

Data that will be deleted:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number, photo)
  • Your event registrations and attendance records
  • Your support tickets and messages
  • Your article likes, reactions, comments, and reading history
  • Your magazine and video engagement history
  • Your bookmarks and saved content
  • Your discussion posts, poll votes, and Q&A submissions
  • Your uploaded event photos
  • Your verification status and documents
  • Your notification preferences and history
  • Your newsletter subscription
  • Your active login sessions

Before deleting, you can export a copy of all your data in JSON format from Profile → Export My Data.
