Privacy Policy

burundi4africa.com

1. Introduction

Welcome to the B4africa application ("the App"), operated through burundi4africa.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application dedicated to Burundi's Chairmanship of the African Union 2025–2026.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Profile Information: Phone number, gender, nationality, date of birth, and profile photo (optional).
  • Verification Data: Professional title, position, organization, country, gender, reasoning message, and social media links (Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube) if you apply for badge verification.
  • Support Tickets: Messages and optional screenshot attachments when you contact support.
  • Event Registrations: Full name, email, phone number, gender, and custom form data when registering for events. If you register a proxy attendee, their information is also collected.
  • Event Feedback: Ratings and feedback you submit after attending events.
  • Community Content: Discussion posts, replies, poll votes, Q&A questions, and comments you submit within the App.
  • Event Photos: Photos you upload to event photo galleries.
  • Newsletter Preference: Whether you opt in or out of email newsletters.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device brand, model, operating system version, and app version. This information is sent to our server when you use the App.
  • Push Notification Tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device tokens to deliver push notifications about events and news.
  • Online Status: The App sends periodic heartbeat signals to our server while the App is in the foreground to indicate your device is online.
  • Content Engagement: We record which articles, magazines, videos, and gallery albums you view, as well as likes, reactions, and comments. For articles, we also track reading progress (scroll position and completion percentage).
  • Notification Engagement: We track when notifications are delivered, displayed, opened, or dismissed on your device.
  • Search Queries: Search terms you enter when searching for articles or magazines within the App.
  • Bookmarks: Content you bookmark is stored on our server and linked to your account.
  • Crash Reports: Crash logs and diagnostic data via Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix bugs. This may include stack traces, device state, and app state at the time of the crash.
  • Error Tracking: The App uses Sentry.io for error monitoring. When an error occurs, Sentry may capture stack traces, app state, a screenshot of the current screen, and the view hierarchy. Sentry does not receive your name or email address.
  • Active Sessions: We track your active login sessions, including device type and login time, so you can view and revoke sessions from your account settings.

2.3 Information Collected With Your Permission

The App requests certain device permissions. These are only used when you explicitly grant them and can be revoked at any time in your device settings:

  • Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION): Approximate or precise location to show nearby embassies and events. Location data is used only while the App is open and is not tracked in the background.
  • Camera (CAMERA): To take a profile photo, capture event photos, or scan QR codes. No images are captured without your action.
  • Photo Library (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): To select photos from your gallery for your profile or event uploads. Only photos you explicitly choose are accessed.
  • Save to Photo Library (NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription on iOS): To save photos from the App to your device's photo library.
  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS): To share embassy contact information directly to your contacts. Contact data is read on-device only and is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Calendar (NSCalendarsUsageDescription on iOS): To add Chairmanship events and meeting reminders to your personal calendar. Calendar data is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): To send you push notifications about Chairmanship news, events, and updates.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Financial or payment information
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA or Google Advertising ID)
  • Browsing history outside the App
  • Background location tracking

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's features and services
  • To authenticate your account and personalize your experience
  • To verify your identity for badge verification requests
  • To send push notifications about AU Chairmanship news and events (with your permission)
  • To send email newsletters (if you opt in)
  • To process and respond to support tickets
  • To send email notifications about support ticket replies and account activity
  • To verify your email address via one-time password (OTP) during sign-up
  • To track content engagement and improve the relevance of content
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App
  • To identify and fix technical issues via crash reports and error tracking
  • To manage event registrations, waitlists, and attendance

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may process your information:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, cloud messaging (push notifications), crash reporting (Crashlytics), and remote configuration (Firebase Privacy Policy). Firebase SDKs may collect default analytics data such as app installs, sessions, and first-open events.
  • Google Sign-In — Optional authentication method. When used, we receive your Google account name, email, and profile photo (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Apple Sign-In — Optional authentication method. When used, we receive your Apple ID and optionally your name and email (Apple Privacy Policy)
  • Sentry.io — Error tracking and performance monitoring. Sentry receives crash data, stack traces, and may capture screenshots and view hierarchy when errors occur. Sentry does not receive personally identifiable information such as your name or email (Sentry Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudflare — Content delivery, DNS, and security (Cloudflare Privacy Policy)
  • DigitalOcean — Server hosting and data storage (DigitalOcean Privacy Policy)
  • Google Maps — Directions to embassy and event locations

5. Cookies and Local Storage

The App itself does not use browser cookies. However:

  • Authentication Tokens: JWT access and refresh tokens are stored in encrypted platform-specific secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain) on your device.
  • Local Preferences: App settings such as language preference (English/French) and theme (Light/Dark) are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • Local Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (screens visited, features used, session counts) are stored locally on your device and are never sent to our servers. You can clear this data at any time.
  • Web Pages: When you visit our web pages (such as this privacy policy) through a browser, standard server logs may record your IP address and browser information. Our hosting infrastructure (Cloudflare, DigitalOcean) may set essential cookies for security and performance.
  • Firebase: Firebase SDKs may store identifiers locally on your device for messaging and crash reporting functionality.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS.
  • The App uses certificate pinning in production to prevent man-in-the-middle attacks.
  • Authentication tokens are stored in encrypted platform-specific secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain).
  • Passwords are hashed using Django's PBKDF2 algorithm and are never stored in plain text.
  • Our servers enforce HSTS (HTTP Strict Transport Security) with a one-year duration.
  • API endpoints are protected with rate limiting to prevent abuse.
  • Media files (profile photos, attachments) are stored on encrypted DigitalOcean Spaces with access controls.
  • Database backups are encrypted and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • The App prevents screenshots and screen recording on sensitive screens to protect your information.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Deactivated Accounts: If you deactivate your account ("Take a Break"), your data is preserved and your account can be reactivated by logging in.
  • Deleted Accounts: When you request account deletion, your account is scheduled for permanent deletion after a 30-day grace period. During this period, you can cancel by logging in. After 30 days, all your data — including your profile, content engagement history, event registrations, support tickets, verification records, discussion posts, bookmarks, and uploaded photos — is permanently and irreversibly deleted.
  • Content Engagement: Article views, reading progress, likes, and reactions are retained for the duration of your account.
  • Event Registrations: Retained for the duration of your account and deleted when your account is deleted.
  • Support Tickets: Retained for the duration of your account for audit and quality purposes.
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 90 days (Firebase Crashlytics and Sentry.io).
  • Push Notification Tokens: Deactivated on logout and deleted with your account.

8. Your Rights (Including GDPR)

Regardless of where you are located, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You can view and export the personal data we hold about you at any time using the "Export My Data" feature in the App. This produces a machine-readable JSON file containing your profile, verification records, event registrations, support tickets, and engagement data.
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct inaccurate personal data via your Profile settings in the App.
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You can request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data via Account settings or by emailing us. Deletion completes after a 30-day grace period.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can deactivate your account temporarily ("Take a Break"), which suspends active processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: The "Export My Data" feature provides your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable JSON format.
  • Right to Object: You can opt out of push notifications through your device settings, unsubscribe from newsletters, and revoke location, camera, contacts, calendar, or photo library permissions at any time.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app features or contact us at info@burundi4africa.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
