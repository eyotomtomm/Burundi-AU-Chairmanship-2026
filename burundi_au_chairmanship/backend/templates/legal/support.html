Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the app and tap "Sign Up". You can register with your email address or sign in with Google or Apple. After registration, you'll receive an OTP to verify your email.

How do I get a verification badge?

Go to your Profile and tap "Get Verified". Complete the 5-step verification process including email verification, phone verification, and social media proof. Our team will review your application.

How do I register for events?

Navigate to the Calendar tab, select an event, and tap "Register". Fill in the required fields and submit your registration. You can also register a proxy attendee.

How do I watch live feeds?

Go to Live Feeds from the home screen. Active streams will show a "LIVE" badge. Tap to watch directly in the app or join via Zoom, Teams, or other platforms.

How do I change the language?

Go to More → Language and toggle between English and French. The app fully supports both languages.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → scroll down to "Delete Account". Your data will be soft-deleted for 30 days (in case you change your mind) and then permanently removed. You can also export your data before deletion.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use HTTPS encryption, secure token storage, and certificate pinning in production. We do not share your personal data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.
