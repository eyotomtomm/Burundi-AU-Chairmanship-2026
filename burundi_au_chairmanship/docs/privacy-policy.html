Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Burundi4africa ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application dedicated to Burundi's African Union Chairmanship 2025-2026.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Profile Information: Any additional profile details you choose to provide.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as screens visited, feature usage, and session duration. This helps us improve the App.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs and diagnostic data to identify and fix bugs.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and debugging purposes.
  • Push Notification Tokens: If you enable notifications, we collect your device token to send relevant updates about AU Chairmanship events and news.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise location data
  • Contact lists or phone numbers
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, videos, or files from your device

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's features
  • To authenticate your account and personalize your experience
  • To send push notifications about AU Chairmanship news and events (with your permission)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App
  • To identify and fix technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase Analytics - Usage analytics (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Firebase Crashlytics - Crash reporting
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging - Push notifications
  • Firebase Authentication - User authentication
  • Google Maps - Directions to embassy locations

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. Account data is stored on our servers and protected with encryption. We use JSON Web Tokens (JWT) for secure authentication.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Analytics and crash data are retained according to Firebase's default retention policies. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings
  • Withdraw consent at any time

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
