Account & Data Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your Burundi4africa account and all associated data by following these steps:

  1. From within the app: Go to More tab → Profile → Delete Account. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
  2. By email: Send an email to burundi4africa@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.
Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed and you will need to create a new account to use the app again.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Data Type Action Timeframe
Account information (name, email) Deleted Within 30 days
Authentication credentials Deleted Immediately
Profile data Deleted Within 30 days
Push notification tokens Deleted Immediately
Analytics data Anonymized & retained Already anonymous
Crash reports Retained (non-personal) Expires per Firebase policy

Analytics and crash data are collected anonymously and cannot be linked back to your account after deletion.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. During this period, your account will be deactivated and inaccessible. After 30 days, all personal data is permanently removed from our systems.
